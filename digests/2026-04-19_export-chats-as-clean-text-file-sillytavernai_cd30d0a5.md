### TL;DR
A SillyTavernAI Reddit thread asks how to export chats as a cleaner text format for fine-tuning, and top commenter `u/Sienne_` says you can use **View Past Chats** → **Download chat as plain text document** from the lower-left menu.

### Key Quote
"Click the menu icon on the lowest left (where you type a message). Click View Past Chats. Then on the right, click the icon for Download chat as plain text document."

- **Thread topics:**

  - Preparing chat logs as fine-tuning data for NovelAI

- Original poster `u/Monkey_1505` wanted a format like:
  - `Char name: thing they did or said`
  - `Other Char Name: thing they did or said`
- Their goal was to use chat history as a **fine-tuning data source** for NovelAI.
- They noted that:
  - the existing **JSON files are too noisy**
  - raw text often loses speaker attribution, leading to ambiguous lines like `"I did this"` without saying who acted or spoke
- `u/Sienne_` answered with a simple UI path in SillyTavern:
  - open the **menu icon** at the lower left
  - click **View Past Chats**
  - on the right side, click **Download chat as plain text document**
