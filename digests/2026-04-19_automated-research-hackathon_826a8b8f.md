### TL;DR
Paradigm’s “Automated Research Hackathon” is a $9,000-prize event split into online challenges, in-person challenges, and an in-person-only projects track focused on automated research, trading/game prompts, and performance engineering.

### Key Quote
“Open-endedSubmit a GitHub link to your automated research project.”

### Summary
- **Event/host**
  - Hosted by **Paradigm**.
  - Title: **Automated Research Hackathon**.

- **Prizes**
  - Total prize pool: **$9,000**.
  - Split into three equal pools:
    - **$3k** for **Online challenges**
    - **$3k** for **In-person challenges**
    - **$3k** for **Projects track**

- **Challenges**
  - **Negotiation**  
    - **Natural Language** challenge.
    - Prompt an AI agent to negotiate resource splits against a baseline.
    - Format: **10 games**.
    - Winner: **highest mean score**.
  - **Prediction Market**  
    - **Python** challenge.
    - Write a Python market-making strategy for a limit order book.
    - Goal: capture edge from retail flow while avoiding the arbitrageur.
    - Format: **50 simulations**.
  - **Attention Kernel**  
    - **Python** challenge.
    - Build the fastest **numerically faithful block-sparse attention backend** for **H100**.

- **Projects track**
  - **Open-ended** submission.
  - Requires a **GitHub link** to an automated research project.
  - Any project related to automated research is allowed, including:
    - harnesses
    - tools
    - benchmarks
    - challenges
    - demonstrations
  - **Only in-person participants** are eligible for prizes or presentations.
