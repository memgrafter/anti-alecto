worked fine.I encountered an issue while trying to publish with Npm, as the process seemed to be hung up and unresponsive. After several hours of troubleshooting, I discovered that the problem was being caused by the watch mode being enabled in the webpack.config.json file with the configuration of 'watch: true'. Once I removed this configuration, the publishing process completed successfully without any further issues.
