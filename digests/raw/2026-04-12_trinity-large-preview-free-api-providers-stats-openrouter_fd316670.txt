OpenRouter supports reasoning-enabled models that can show their step-by-step thinking process. Use the reasoning
parameter in your request to enable reasoning, and access the reasoning_details
array in the response to see the model's internal reasoning before the final answer. When continuing a conversation, preserve the complete reasoning_details
when passing messages back to the model so it can continue reasoning from where it left off. Learn more about reasoning tokens.
