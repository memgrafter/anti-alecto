Not a sandbox.
Cloud sandboxes limit what you can do. Terminal-only tools limit what you can see. AFK gives you your full environment — browser, simulator, databases, running services.
Peer-to-peer WebRTC
Your phone connects directly to your Mac. Screen data and input never touch our servers.
Open source host

End-to-end encrypted
All traffic encrypted with DTLS-SRTP. Our signaling server only helps your devices find each other.
No account needed
Pair once with a code. Session keys stay on your devices. Just install and connect.
