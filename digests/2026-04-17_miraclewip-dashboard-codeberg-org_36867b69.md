### TL;DR
Codeberg is a nonprofit, community-run Git hosting platform for free/open-source projects, emphasizing privacy, European hosting, and support for the commons over commercialization.

### Key Quote
"Free as in freedom, not as in beer!"

### Summary
- **What it is**
  - Codeberg describes itself as “Software development, but free!”
  - It provides services for free and open-source projects, including:
    - Git hosting using **Forgejo**
    - **Pages**
    - **CI/CD**
    - A **Weblate** instance

- **Organizational model**
  - Operated by **Codeberg e.V.**, a **non-profit** organization based in **Berlin, Germany**
  - Says its mission is to support the commons and keep the service in the hands of its users
  - Encourages people to join as **supporting or active members** with voting rights

- **Community positioning**
  - Frames Codeberg as more than a hosting service: a community of developers, artists, academics, hobbyists, and professionals
  - Emphasizes values of:
    - free culture
    - openness
    - creativity

- **Privacy / trust claims**
  - Claims:
    - **No tracking**
    - **No third-party cookies**
    - **No profiteering**
    - Data is not sold
  - Says everything runs on servers they control
  - Notes it is **hosted in Europe** and “welcomes the world”

- **Funding and sustainability**
  - States that maintaining systems and developing software costs money
  - Says the service is backed by **optional donations**
  - Encourages donations to help improve service quality and sustain the mission
  - Invites contributions to Codeberg’s own projects and services
