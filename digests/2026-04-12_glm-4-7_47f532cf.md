### TL;DR
GLM-4.7 is Fireworks’ next-generation general-purpose model for coding, reasoning, and agentic workflows, notable for its advanced “thinking” controls and support for serverless, on-demand, and fine-tuning use.

### Key Quote
“advanced thinking controls: interleaved, preserved, and turn-level thinking; to improve stability on long-horizon, multi-turn tasks.”

### Summary
- **What it is**
  - GLM-4.7 is presented as a next-generation **general-purpose model**.
  - It is optimized for:
    - **Coding**
    - **Reasoning**
    - **Agentic workflows**
  - Fireworks claims it delivers strong gains in:
    - **Multilingual software engineering**
    - **Tool use**
    - **Complex problem solving**

- **Main distinguishing feature**
  - The model introduces **advanced thinking controls**:
    - **Interleaved thinking**
    - **Preserved thinking**
    - **Turn-level thinking**
  - These are intended to improve **stability on long-horizon, multi-turn tasks**.
