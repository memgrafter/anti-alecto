### TL;DR
A Lobsters discussion debates whether Kagi’s search quality comes from its own paid sources or from third-party SERP providers, with commenters pointing to Kagi’s help docs and asking how its sources compare to Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, and Brave.

- The thread closes with a comparative question about source quality and tradeoffs:
