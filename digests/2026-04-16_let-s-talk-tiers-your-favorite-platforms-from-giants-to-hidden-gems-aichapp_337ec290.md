A Reddit thread in r/aichapp asks users to rank AI chat platforms as giants, underrated, or hidden gems, prompting replies that name Character.AI, WyvernChat, erogen.ai, The AI Peeps, and Companion Labs with brief feature-based justifications.

### Key Quote
“We all have our favorites,but they don't all have the same size of user base.”

### Summary
- **Original post:** asks the community to sort their favorite AI platforms into three tiers:
  - **The Giants**: mainstream platforms everyone knows
  - **The Underrated**: medium-sized platforms that deserve more recognition
  - **The Hidden Gems**: small/new platforms with potential
- The prompt is specifically framed as a discovery/share-your-find thread for AI chatbot platforms.

- **Prominent named platforms and claims from comments:**
  - **Character.AI** is cited as the “giant” by one user, who says it still sets the standard for:
    - core models
    - dialogue flow
    - overall user experience
    - They still revisit it “once in a while out of nostalgia.”
  - **erogen.ai** is described as an “underrated” platform because:
    - personalities stay consistent
    - memory tracks relationships better
    - it focuses on immersion rather than gimmicks
  - **WyvernChat** is presented as “underrated” by its own representative, who claims:
    - free, unlimited chatting
    - AI models in the **12B–32B** range
    - **SillyTavern-level customizability**
    - group chat, multiplayer chat, lorebooks, personas, character galleries, sprites, and chat branching
    - parent company is an LLM inference provider, so the site is framed as less vulnerable to payment-platform pressure or “sudden rug pulls”
    - it supports **BYOK** from many providers, including **OpenRouter, Deepseek, OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, Infermatic, and ArliAI**, plus any OAI-compatible endpoint
  - **The AI Peeps** is called “DEFO UNDERRATED” by another commenter, who emphasizes:
    - strong performance
    - no filter
    - high-quality image and video generation
  - **Companion Labs** is mentioned by the OP as a platform they are “personally impressed by,” with:
    - a **credit system**
    - **PAYG** usage
    - an API key provided automatically to choose a model

- **Discussion tone / smaller side remarks:**
  - The thread reads like both a recommendation exchange and light platform promotion.

  - The OP also makes a side comment that the chatbot user base seems gender-skewed, with women tending toward Character.AI or Chai, and men toward Kindroid; they also mention having checked out CrushOn and Soulkyn, and not being interested in Replika.
