### TL;DR
DigitalOcean promo codes give one-time, non-transferable team credits that require a valid payment method, expire after 1 year in most cases, and cannot be used for SaaS Add-Ons.

### Key Quote
“Each team can redeem one promo code.”

### Summary
- **Purpose:** Promo codes let you apply credits to a DigitalOcean team.
- **Prerequisite:** Your team must have a **valid payment method** to redeem a promo code and create Droplets or other resources.
- **How to redeem:**
  - Go to your team’s **Billing** page.
  - In the **Overview** tab, find the **Promo code** section.
  - Enter the **case-sensitive** code.
  - Click **Apply Code**.
- **After redemption:**
  - Credits are automatically applied to the team.
  - The **Promo code** section disappears from the Billing page.
  - Team credits are visible in the **control panel** and on **invoices**.
- **Limits and restrictions:**
  - **One promo code per team**: if a team has redeemed one before, it cannot redeem another.
  - Promo code credits are **non-transferable** to other teams.
  - Credits usually **expire after 1 year**.
  - Some promotional credits expire after **60 days**.
  - Promo codes do **not apply to SaaS Add-Ons**.
- **History/support:**
  - Promo code history is visible in the **Billing history** section.
  - DigitalOcean says to **contact support** if you have questions about a specific promotional credit.
